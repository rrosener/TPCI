Read me for vary_nete

 

vary_nete.cpp

This is an example of a calculation in which two parameters, the electron density and gas kinetic temperature, are varied over a broad range.  Relative intensities of several [O III] lines are predicted.

This figure shows the [O III] 4363 / 5007 ratio as a function of density and temperature, as output from this program.
