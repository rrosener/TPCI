Read me for Hazy cooling curve sample program

This is an example of a gas in collisional ionization equilibrium in which only the temperature is varied.  The gas temperature is varied over a very broad range and only the cosmic background and cosmic rays are included.  The hydrogen density and gas kinetic temperature are specified and a single zone is computed.  The temperature and cooling divided by n(H)2 are printed.  This was used to create a figure in Hazy.

This figure shows the total cooling over the density squared..  The x-axis is the log of the gas temperature (K).  The y-axis is the cooling divided by the square of the density (erg s-1 cm3).
